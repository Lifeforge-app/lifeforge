<p align="center">A comprehensive personal management system to streamline and organize all aspects of your life.</p>

## The Problem

- Managing various aspects of life can be overwhelming. üò©
- Lack of a centralized system to keep track of tasks, projects, and personal goals. ü§Ø
- Difficulty in integrating different tools and resources into one cohesive platform. üõ†Ô∏è

## The Solution

Lifeforge is a personal management system designed to help you manage your life efficiently. Whether it's keeping track of tasks, organizing projects, or setting personal goals, Lifeforge has got you covered. 

## üåü Features

- **Dashboard**: Get an overview of your entire life in one place.
- **Task Management**: Create, organize, and prioritize tasks with ease.
- **Project Tracking**: Keep track of your projects and their progress.
- **Idea Box**: Store and organize your ideas for future projects.
- **Todo List**: Manage your daily tasks and stay on top of your to-do list.
- **Calendar**: Keep track of important dates and events.
- **Spotify Integration**: Listen to your favorite music while managing your tasks.
- **Code Time**: Track and manage your coding time.
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Use the Pomodoro technique to enhance your productivity.
- **Flashcards**: Create and study flashcards for better retention.
- **Notes**: Take and organize notes efficiently.
- **Books Library**: Keep track of your reading list and favorite books.
- **Journal**: Maintain a personal journal to reflect on your daily activities.
- **Achievements**: Track and celebrate your achievements.
- **Wallet**: Manage your finances, including balance, transactions, budgets, and reports.
- **Wish List**: Keep track of items you wish to purchase.
- **Photos**: A replica of Google Photos but better!
- **Music**: Access and manage your music library.
- **Repositories**: Keep track of your coding projects and repositories.
- **Mail Inbox**: Manage your emails efficiently.
- **DNS Records**: Manage DNS records for your websites.
- **Blog Posts**: Write and manage your blog posts.
- **Contacts**: Store and organize your contacts.
- **Passwords**: Securely manage your passwords.
- **Personalization**: Customize the appearance and functionality of Lifeforge.
- **Modules**: Enable or disable various modules to suit your needs.
- **Data Backup**: Backup and restore your data.
- **Server Status**: Monitor the status of your server.
- **Change Log**: Keep track of changes and updates to Lifeforge.
- **About**: Learn more about Lifeforge and its creators.
